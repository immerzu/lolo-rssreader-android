RSS Reader is a compact, classic RSS reader for Android.

The app collects RSS and Atom feeds in one place and shows new articles in a clean, compact list. New posts are stored locally and can be read in the built-in reader view or opened in the browser.

Features:

* Subscribe to RSS and Atom feeds
* Import and export OPML
* Mark articles as read or unread
* Protect favorites from deletion
* Light and dark theme
* Background refresh with notifications
* Compact reader view with images and swipe navigation
* Optional article translation via a Google web service

The app is designed for straightforward and fast feed management.
