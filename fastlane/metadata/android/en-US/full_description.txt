RSS Reader is a compact, classic RSS reader for Android.

The app collects RSS and Atom feeds in one place and displays new articles in a clear, compact list view. New entries are stored locally and can be read directly in the integrated reader view or opened in the browser.

Features:

- Subscribe to RSS and Atom feeds
- Import and export OPML files
- Mark articles as read or unread
- Protect favorites from deletion
- Light and dark mode
- Background updates with notifications
- Compact reader view with images and swipe navigation

The app is designed for simple, fast, and organized feed management.
